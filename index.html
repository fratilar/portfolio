What I Do

Frontend-Development

I started learning frontend development in 2019 when I discovered my passion for it. From then until February 2020, I learned on my own, and in March of the same year I started the Web Designer course organized by Azimut Vision, which I finished in September. During the course I learned the basics of HTML5, CSS3, Javascript and WordPress.

Freelancing

Technological evolution allows us to work practically from anywhere we want, so I signed up for the freelancing website, upwork.com, to experience this part of being a freelancer. What I can say is that at the beginning it is very difficult to get contracts being a beginner and not having experience.

Web Design

I also experimented with the Adobe XD application in which I made images of two of the websites I made and I can say that I do quite well. I can say that after a few tutorials I learned to work in Adobe XD and I can make templates of websites with it.

WordPress

My first WordPress website was created in May 2020, an e-commerce website, the site of a restaurant where you can order food online. This site was one of the projects that were needed to pass the exam. Since then I have made another portfolio website with WordPress from scratch, only with Elementor.
